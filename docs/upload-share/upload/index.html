Uploading 3D CAD Models

This tutorial will guide you through the process of uploading a file to your workspace.

  1. Begin by clicking at the top of the sidebar to select the appropriate organization.
  1. Then, select the desired workspace.

  1. Next, click on the + icon and select Add New File.

  1. Click on Browse to choose the file you wish to upload.

  1. The selected file will then be added to the workspace and the designated folder.
  1. You can double-click the file to open the model page.

  1. To preview the uploaded model view, click on Explore.

By following these steps, you will successfully upload a file to your workspace and explore its content.
